Customer Churn Analysis using Power BI(Task 2)

This project analyzes customer churn behavior using Power BI dashboards and business analytics techniques.

Project Objectives:
> Understand customer churn patterns
> Identify high-risk customer segments
> Analyze contract type, tenure, and monthly charges impact
> Provide business recommendations to reduce churn

Tools Used:
> Power BI
> Data Cleaning & Transformation
> DAX Measures
> Data Visualization

Key Metrics:
> Total Customers: 7000+
>Churn Customers: 1800+
> Churn Rate: 27%
> Average Tenure: 32 months

Insights:
> Month-to-month contracts show highest churn
> Long-term contracts reduce churn significantly
> Higher monthly charges correlate with higher churn risk

Business Recommendations:
> Encourage long-term subscriptions
> Provide loyalty benefits for early-tenure customers
> Offer retention discounts for high-risk users
